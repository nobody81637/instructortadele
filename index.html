About Tadele Workineh

With over 17 years of experience in the fitness and wellness industry, Tadele Workineh is a results-driven personal trainer known for his holistic, client-centered approach. He has successfully worked with a wide range of clients—from beginners to elite professionals—helping them achieve transformative results through personalized fitness, clinical guidance, and nutritional support. Tadele holds more than 35 certifications across disciplines including strength training, clinical fitness, rehabilitation, sports performance, and nutrition coaching. His in-depth knowledge allows him to blend science-backed training with practical lifestyle solutions, offering clients a complete wellness experience. In addition to his fitness credentials, Tadele holds a degree in Social Anthropology from Rift Valley University, which enriches his understanding of individual behavior, motivation, and the cultural aspects of health and wellness. This unique academic background enhances his ability to connect with clients on a deeper level and support lasting behavioral change. Tadele’s professional journey includes working with premier institutions such as the Rori Hotel, delivering high-quality training to an exclusive clientele. With his unmatched expertise, motivational coaching style, and commitment to excellence, Tadele Workineh stands out as a trusted leader in fitness, health, and personal transformation.
